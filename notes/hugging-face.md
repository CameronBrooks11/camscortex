# Hugging Face

Hugging Face is an AI company specializing in natural language processing (NLP). They provide a platform for sharing machine learning models, datasets, and tools, making it easier for developers and researchers to build and deploy AI applications. Hugging Face is known for its open-source libraries like Transformers, which offer pre-trained models for various NLP tasks such as text classification, translation, and question answering. The company aims to democratize AI by fostering collaboration and accessibility within the AI community.
