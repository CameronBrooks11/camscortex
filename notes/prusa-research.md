# Prusa Research

Prusa Research is a well-known manufacturer of 3D printers, founded by Josef Prusa. The company is renowned for its open-source designs and high-quality printers, particularly the Prusa i3 series. In more recent years, Prusa Research has expanded its product line to include the SL1 resin printer, the XL large-format toolchanger printer, and the newest Core One model.
