# High Profit Margins for Medical Equipment

- Medical equipment is often expensive due to profit margins. Medical device companies have tremendous profit margins due to the cost of research and development, the high cost of regulatory approval, the cost of production, the need to recoup marketing costs, and the protection of their intellectual property (which impacts pricing).
